Privacy Policy

How SCAI collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account we collect your name, username, and email address. When you use SCAI we store the documents you upload and your conversation history so we can provide the service.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate SCAI: answering your questions from your documents, maintaining your conversation history, enforcing usage quotas, and processing subscriptions. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Document Privacy

Uploaded documents are indexed only for your own queries. Document content is shared with our language-model provider strictly to generate answers to your questions and is not used for any other purpose.

Data Retention

You can delete your conversation history and uploaded documents at any time. When you delete your account, associated data is removed from our systems.

Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data, including secure session management and role-based access to stored information.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at suggestions@scai.co.
